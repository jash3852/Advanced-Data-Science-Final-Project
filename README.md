## Research Questions

1) How much speedup does a Numba-optimized MinHash implementation achieve compared to exact Jaccard similarity for large-text plagiarism detection?

2) How accurately does Minhash approximate Jaccard similarity across news articles?

3) Can MinHash effectively identify highly similar/ potentially plagiarized news articles within large datasets?

4) Do articles within the same category exhibit higher similarity than randomly selected articles?
